List of 3D Assets#

3D assets used in LychSim simulations, including models of 3D scenes, rigid objects, animals, vehicles, and buildings.

Scenes (Indoor)#

  • Residence.
    • Stylized House Interior recommended

      • UE5 Fab

      • Available in Stylized_House_Interior.zip

    • House Props & Furniture Vol. 2 recommended

      • UE5 Fab

      • Available in House_Props_Furniture_Vol2.zip

    • House Interior 2 recommended

      • UE5 Fab

      • Available in House_Interior_2.zip

      • Need to adjust exposure compensation to 1.0: (1) select PostProcessVolume in the World Outliner; (2) in the Details panel, search for Exposure; (3) set Exposure Compensation to 1.0.

    • House Furniture known issue

      • UE5 Fab

      • Available in House_Furniture.zip

  • Warehouse.
    • Factory Interior + Warehouse Props Vol 1 - BUNDLE recommended

      • UE5 Fab

      • Available in Factory_Interior_Warehouse_Props_Bundle.zip

    • Warehouse Environment

      • UE5 Fab

      • Available in Warehouse_Environment.zip

    • Modular Warehouse

      • UE5 Fab

      • Available in Modular_Warehouse.zip

Rigid Objects#

  • Car.
    • Velocity Cars Pack articulation recommended

      • UE5 Fab

      • Available in Velocity_Cars_Pack.zip
